Lore

In mid 2024, i started drawing these fantasy creaures when i was bored in class, they consisted of new ideas that i had in my mind and also some old ones that i've drawn in the start of 2024, originally i didn't have a huge selection of pencils since i was not sure if i would keep up with drawing, until...


An Upgarde

...I got a notebook, some new colored pencils and even some neon ones! This is because i drew over 50 miniatures, so my parents bought me some much needed equipment, i started drawing more and more drawings that i were proud of...


...The 100th eye

Well, i didn't tell you one thing, the things i mostly draw were eyes, but it was time to switch it up, before that i was planning to draw my 100th one [it is still in the making, around 30% done] i also moved on to different objects other than those, and revamped some old ones, i also stared drawing on whole A4 papers instead of miniature ones.


Future plans

Now eventually i'll move on to more realistic things, but im just hooked on drawing these because it had evolved my drawings skills while also making some stunning visuals :D
